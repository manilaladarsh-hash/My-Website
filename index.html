Bridging Theory & Experiment

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Physics & Astronomy at McMaster University. My research utilizes Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) and Nuclear Quadrupole Resonance (NQR) to investigate Strongly Correlated Electron Systems.

My work focuses on Quantum Magnetism and Quantum Phase Transitions, specifically studying the electronic properties and phase transitions in High-Temperature Superconductors (HTSC).
